Order integers first.

Add half of the largest half of numbers to the sum (Representing lost regions).
Add all of the least half of numbers to the sum (Representing won regions).

Example

7 7 5 3 1

7 6 5 is the larger half

Add 3, 3 and 2 ( (number-1) / 2).

4 1 is the smaller half

Add 4 and 1

3+3+2+4+1 = 13
