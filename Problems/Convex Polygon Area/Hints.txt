Use the first point as an anchor point.
Then iterate through every two consecutive points and add to area.

You can use this formula for calculating the area of a triangle with 3 coordinate points.

(1/2)*abs((xa-xc)*(yb-ya)-(xa-xb)*(yc-ya))
